# Sprint Retrospective

## What went well

- We delivered a larger MVP v1 increment than initially expected, including the admin interface, backend, database, camera flow, recognition events, and dashboard data.
- The selected user stories were decomposed into concrete PBIs with acceptance criteria, story points, assignees, and reviewers.
- The customer review was positive, and the customer confirmed that the project is moving in the right direction.

## What did not go well

- The recognition model refresh is still not automatic after authorized-person changes, so the current workflow needs a restart or rebuild workaround.
- The project still needs stronger Linux/Ubuntu verification before Raspberry Pi deployment.
- Some reporting artifacts and evidence links were updated late, which created extra cleanup work before submission.

## Action points

- Stabilize and smoke-test the complete MVP v1 workflow on Ubuntu before moving further with Raspberry Pi.
- Keep submission evidence updated during the Sprint: PR links, screenshots, release link, deployment link, demo link, and customer-review artifacts.
