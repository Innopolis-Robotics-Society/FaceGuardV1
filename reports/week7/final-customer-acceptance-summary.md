launch/use the project. The customer also confirmed that the project was ready,
visually acceptable, and working.

The team discussed current constraints: the recognition model is local/offline
and intentionally small because of Raspberry Pi storage and compute limits. The
team also noted that future improvements could use more powerful hardware or
larger models.

## Acceptance Outcome

local/private-network use.

deployment, public release publication, private credential transfer, or physical
Raspberry Pi LED validation beyond what is shown in the available meeting
materials.
